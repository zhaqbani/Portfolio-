Who am I?

I am from Paktia, Afghanistan, and grew up where few girls were encouraged to study. I became the first girl in my village to complete high school, and soon, the first to enter university. As Vice President of my school’s Computer Club, I learned how much I enjoy problem-solving and building things that work. I also stay active through running and once completed a marathon—endurance matters. I launched an initiative helping over 10,000 Afghan girls access Coursera; 300+ completed several courses. I aim to study CS/IT and build Dari/Pashto tools that support women’s health and access to information.
